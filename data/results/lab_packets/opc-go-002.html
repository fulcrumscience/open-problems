opc-go-002

Validate Safe-Harbor Loci and Insulator Sequences for Stable Transgene Expression in Bioenergy Crops

READY TO TEST Score 0.527 $15,000 – $35,000

Problem mapping

Problem: How promoters, enhancers, and cis-regulatory elements (CREs) impact position effects of transgene insertions in bioenergy crops is not understood, and sequences with insulator-like function to dampen ectopic interactions are needed.

Sub-question: Can validated safe harbor loci be identified in bioenergy crop genomes for predictable transgene expression?

Objective

Identify insertion sites and boundary elements that produce high, stable, low-variance expression across tissues and generations.

Readouts

  • Primary: reporter mean expression and coefficient of variation across independent events
  • Primary: expression stability across tissues and T1 generation
  • Secondary: insertion fidelity and copy number
  • Secondary: growth/fitness neutrality

Experimental design

Overview: Head-to-head targeted insertion experiment comparing 3 candidate safe-harbor loci and 2 insulator configurations using a dual-luciferase reporter cassette.

  • WP1 candidate loci nomination from pan-genome and chromatin context
  • WP2 build cassettes: no-insulator, insulator-A, insulator-B
  • WP3 CRISPR-directed insertion into each candidate locus
  • WP4 random-integration comparator arm (standard Agrobacterium transformation)
  • WP5 expression profiling by dual-luciferase and qPCR in controlled growth conditions
  • WP6 multi-tissue and generation follow-up (T0 leaf/callus + T1 seedling tissues)

Controls:

  • Random integration arm as baseline position-effect comparator
  • No-insulator cassette at each candidate locus
  • Constitutive internal Renilla control for ratiometric normalization

Sample size plan: Target >=12 independent insertion events per locus x insulator condition; minimum 3 technical assay replicates per tissue per line.

Success criteria:

  • >=30% reduction in between-line expression CV versus random integration baseline
  • No significant drop in mean expression vs no-insulator comparator at same locus
  • Stable expression rank order maintained in T1 (Spearman rho >=0.7)

Estimated timeline: 24 weeks
